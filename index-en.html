ABOUT ME

Since childhood, I have found in words both a refuge and a compass. Reading, writing and observing the world have always been essential ways to inhabit it with deeper meaning. Over time, this curiosity expanded toward the invisible: the systems and technologies that shape modern life and transform how we connect.

I currently study Computer Engineering, but my identity is not limited to the language of algorithms. I am moved by art, I get lost in books, and I find beauty in a well-framed photograph or in the brushstrokes of a painting that comes to life. Writing —even when silent— remains a way of breathing. It is my inner fire, a promise I do not abandon.

I am passionate about exploring different forms of expression: from code to imagery, from logic to metaphor. I truly believe that sensitivity and analytical thinking are not mutually exclusive, but can coexist and enrich each other. I am drawn to both the world nearby and the one far away, always seeking to understand them better.

Beyond the technical or creative, I deeply value punctuality, discipline and kindness —qualities I strive to bring into every project and challenge.
